Instructions:

Billy the Blogger, a poor soul who can no longer afford wifi (due to poor career choices), must use the free internet facilities of the cafe across the road. Unfortunately he has no peripheral vision and requires help crossing the road.

  1. Use the arrow keys to maneuver Billy.
  2. Your aim is to cross to the road, dodging cars in the process.
  3. Points are awarded when you enter the Internet cafe located at the top of the game screen.
  4. Each successful run awards 10 points which can be found in the top right-hand corner of the screen.
  5. Should you be hit by a car three times in a row, it is game over.
  6. Your lives can be found in the top left-hand corner of the screen.
  7. Good Luck!
